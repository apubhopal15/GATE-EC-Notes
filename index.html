Daily Question Paper Structure

Section Questions Rule
General Aptitude 5 GATE style verbal, reasoning, and quantitative aptitude.
Engineering Mathematics 4 Formula-based and calculation-based questions.
EC Technical 24 Chapter-wise EC mix from networks, signals, control, analog, digital, devices, EMFT, and communication.
Total 33 MCQ main format, GATE EC level or slightly above.

Question Paper Checklist

  • Use clear MCQ options: A, B, C, D.
  • Use original diagram/screenshot source when required.
  • Add one extra option for every question: Question is wrong/incomplete.
  • Do not include personal documents or private student files.
  • Keep the paper compact enough for daily practice.

Submission And Checking

  • Student submits answers through Google Form or answer sheet.
  • Every question is checked: attempted, not attempted, correct, wrong, or wrong/incomplete.
  • Confirmed wrong/incomplete questions are removed from valid scoring.
  • Analysis must explain the correct method and calculation steps.

Scoring

Formula

Percentage

Correct Attempted x 100 / Valid Questions

Pass Mark

60%

60% or above is Pass.

Improvement

Below 60%

Below 60% means Need Improvement with topic-wise practice.
